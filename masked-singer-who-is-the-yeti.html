The fifth season of The Masked Singer has introduced four Wildcards in true game-changing fashion, and they really did save the best for last! Yeti took the stage with a lot of swag and one of the coolest (see what I did there?) costumes we’ve seen all season. But his voice was equally as cool!

So who is the celebrity under the Yeti mask? Let’s piece the clues and voice together to predict who it could be!

Clues on The Masked Singer Season 5

The Yeti’s clues started with him saying he only comes out of hiding “for a mammoth reason.” He’s seen holding a pile of wood, while claiming “where I come from, too many yetis become monsters.” But his “secret weapon” was a village of warrior women who were always there to show him the way.

In the frame, we can see a bunny in front of a paper heart on a bookshelf and a floral centerpiece on a table. We see bread with jelly and berries being added to it. Yeti also made a “Golden Girls” reference. The women then served him three cupcakes, and one of the women blows him a kiss.

After his performance, the men in black caught the judges’ guesses in an ice box. We also got a clue on a shirt that said “Aphrodite.”

He explains, “Yeti is all about love, love, love.” 

Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer Yeti

The panelists have predicted that the Yeti could be Ray J, Justin Bieber, or Taran Killam. But fans online don’t agree with their guesses. They’ve predicted it could be Chris Brown, Omarion, Aaron Carter, Jaleel White, or Marques Houston. It’s definitely up in the air who this Wildcard is!

Who is The Yeti on The Masked Singer?

Now, we knew the voice sounded familiar. But once we sorted through the clues, one name rose above the rest. This is a huge celebrity from the 1990’s! We believe The Masked Singer Yeti is…

Omarion!

If that voice wasn’t enough, he definitely has a lot of connections to The Masked Singer already! Omarion has released music with the Frog (Bow Wow), Robot (Lil Wayne), and Monster (T-Pain) and all the clues point to Omarion too.

When talking about where he’s from, we saw a pile of wood. Omarion is from IngleWOOD. This also explains him saying that where he’s from, “too many yetis become monsters.” Inglewood is in South L.A. and notorious for its gang violence.

The “village of warrior women” could be talking about his three sisters and mom, who was also his stylist while he was in B2K. We saw berries in the clue package too, which point to his last name Grandberry. Yeti saying he’s “all about love” makes sense for Omarion too, since most of his hits are about love.

A few more specific clues point to Omarion too. The bunny with a heart alludes to him being on Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood. The heart represents love and the bunny, hip hop! Very punny. The ice box that the men in black used to collect the first impression guesses also has a direct connection to Omarion. He has a song called “Ice Box” that was one of his biggest singles ever.
