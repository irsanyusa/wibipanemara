Profession: Boxer

Biography: Floyd Mayweather Jr. is an American former professional boxer who achieved an undefeated record during his career with a record of 49–0, with 26 knockouts. Known for his technical skill, defensive prowess, and strategic approach to fighting, Mayweather is often cited as one of the greatest boxers in the history of the sport.

He won world titles in multiple weight classes and fought in some of the highest-grossing pay-per-view events of all time, including bouts against Manny Pacquiao and Conor McGregor. A five-division world champion, Mayweather has won 12 world titles and the championship in four different weight classes.

Mayweather's boxing career was often subject to controversy, including his flamboyant and divisive persona, as well as legal issues outside the ring. Often this only helped his career, making him a pay-per-view attraction that drew massive audiences and generated billions of dollars in revenue.
