The Masked Singer Season 7 is certainly gearing up to be the most dramatic season yet. In a new sneak peek, the McTerrier slips and falls on stage. The contestant loses the head of his mask, accidentally exposing him to the crowd and judges for the first time in The Masked Singer history.

It appears as though the McTerrier slipped after he was finished performing. Trying to keep from falling and hold onto the microphone, the McTerrier knocks the entire mask off his head and into the crowd.

The audience can hear panelist Jenny McCarthy screaming about the incident: “His mask just fell off, turn around Nicole!” All of the judges seem to jump to their feet in shock while trying to avoid seeing the celebrity performer.

McTerrier Tumbles on Stage During The Masked Singer Premiere

After knocking his costume head off, the McTerrier completely falls to the floor. When he gets up, he tried to hide his face from the crowd. In the video, we can hear him asking “What do I do?”

Clearly, the season 7 premiere is going to be the most dramatic episode of The Masked Singer we’ve ever seen. Not only is this the first time a contestant has been accidentally unmasked, fans can expect a controversial appearance tomorrow night.

Rudy Giuliani is expected to be unmasked in the first episode of the season. Last month, Ken Jeong walked off the show’s stage during filming when the notorious politician was revealed. Fellow panelist Robin Thicke also left the set, but the two returned shortly after. According to insiders, the cast was filming the premiere episode at the time.

The Masked Singer season 7 premieres tomorrow at 8 p.m. ET on Fox.
