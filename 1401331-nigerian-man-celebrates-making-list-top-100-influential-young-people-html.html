A Nigerian man identified as Olugbenga Ogunbowale has taken to social media to celebrate his achievement after he was announced as one of the top 100 influential young people by Opportunities Hub.

Ogunbowale said he dedicates the recognition to all young leaders around the world who are displeased with the situation of things around them and are working to ensure a change.

He wrote:

"This recognition is dedicated to all young leaders around the world who are dissatisfied with the status quo and are working to ensure a better future for the world.
