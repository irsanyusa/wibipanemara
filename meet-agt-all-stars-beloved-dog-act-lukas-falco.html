Meet 'AGT All-Stars' Beloved Dog Act Lukas &amp; Falco

Lukas & Falco stepped in America’s Got Talent season 14 as a canine freestyle duo. But before that, this team had already one Got Talent win under their belt, them being winners of Das Supertalent 2013. This year, they will try to snatch another with their adorable dog-centric acts.

Lukas Pratschker, his dog Falco are The Best of Friends

Lukas Pratschker’s love for dogs has been manifested from childhood to his adult years. Since he finished school in 2014, he’s work as a full-time dog trainer in Vienna. He’s also launched School of Dog, a modern dog training center. During weekends, he and Falco show off their musical freestyle at various events around Europe.

Falco, on the other hand, is an extremely talented Border Collie who likes to play with his other brothers, Joke and Turner. As an active yet relaxed dog, he takes long walks in the forest and swims in the lake. Since Lukas’s family owned him, Falco has been his bestfriend.

They Joined America’s Got Talent Season 14

Lukas and Falco are originally from Vienna, Austria and have been doing canine freestyle for nearly a decade. Back in his audition in AGT season 14, he told the judges about their hilarious meet-up.

“When I was a child, my dad and my family all wanted a dog at home. So we met all the puppies, and Falco was peeing on my dad’s leg and I found it funny,” he said. “[I said] ‘we should take him,’ and it was the start of a great friendship.”

They then proceeded to fascinate the audience with their cowboy-themed routine to Kenny Loggins’ “Footloose.” Such performance prompted Simon Cowell to give them a standing ovation, subsequently getting four yeses from all judges. The dog act was eliminated in the Quarterfinals, but they came back in the Semifinals as a wildcard entry. Unfortunately, they got eliminated from the competition after that.

Be that as it may, Lukas and Falco became one of the most memorable acts in season 14. Amazingly, before they joined AGT in 2019, they’ve won the Germany version of Got Talent franchise, Das Supertalent.

Lukas & Falco Are Competing in AGT All-Stars

After AGT season 14, Lukas and Falco continued to perform in various events. Today, he offers new online courses, workshops, and individual coaching sessions in School of Dog.

This year, Lukas and Falco are joining AGT All-Stars. The canine freestyle duo is among the 60 talented acts vying to be the first ever winner of the spinoff series. To witness their journey in the competition, be sure to tune in on NBC.
