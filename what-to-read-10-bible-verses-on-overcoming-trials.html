10 Bible verses on OVERCOMING TRIALS

September 05, 2024
blog

This is a huge misconception.

Being a Christian means having faith in someone, other than yourself.

More importantly, it is knowing that you will make it through trials and tribulations because you have God, in your corner.

ADVERTISEMENT

These 10 bible verses prove this.

1. Psalm 27:1

The LORD is my light and my salvation— whom shall I fear? The LORD is the stronghold of my life— of whom shall I be afraid?

2. Psalm 34:17-18

ADVERTISEMENT

The righteous cry out, and the Lord hears them; He delivers them from all their troubles.

The Lord is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit.

3. Psalm 91:15

He will call on me, and I will answer him;  I will be with him in trouble, I will deliver him and honor him.

4. Psalm 9:9-10

ADVERTISEMENT

The Lord is a refuge for the oppressed, a stronghold in times of trouble.

Those who know your name trust in you, for you, Lord, have never forsaken those who seek you.

5. Psalm 46:1

God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble.

6. James 1:2

ADVERTISEMENT

Count it all joy, my brothers, when you meet trials of various kinds.

7. Romans 12:12

Rejoice in hope, be patient in tribulation, be constant in prayer.

8. James 1:12

Blessed [is] the man that endureth temptation: for when he is tried, he shall receive the crown of life, which the Lord hath promised to them that love him.

ADVERTISEMENT

9. Exodus 14:14

The LORD shall fight for you, and ye shall hold your peace.

10. Matthew 19:26 - But Jesus beheld [them], and said unto them, With men this is impossible; but with God all things are possible.

Use these whenever you faced with life's challenges.
